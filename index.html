Overview

I work at the point where operations and technology meet. As Technical Operations Manager at Go Plane Private Limited, an IATA-accredited travel agency, I keep booking, ticketing, and reporting processes running — and when a process needs a tool that doesn't exist yet, I build it myself instead of waiting on it.

That's produced things like a full commercial invoice and quotation generator handling multi-currency pricing and airline class-wise fares, GoZayaan-style booking confirmation templates, ticket sales analysis across 265+ transactions and 14 airlines, and IATA ticketing-authority documentation for management.

Outside of Go Plane, I work as a WordPress developer across Elementor, Elementor Pro, Wix, and Webflow — building full sites for garments buying houses, engineering suppliers, transport companies, and service businesses across Bangladesh and the US.
